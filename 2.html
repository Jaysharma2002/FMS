INGREDIENTS

Mung dal - 1 bowl (small)

Rice- Œ bowl (small)

Oats- 2 tbsp

Grated carrot- œ bowl (small)

Gratted cabbage - œ bowl (small)

Half onion

Corriander

Cumin seed powder - Œ tsp

Salt to taste



How to prepare

Soak mung dal and rice, separately for 4 at least hours.

Grind them separately and then mix them.

Add cumin seeds and salt in this mixture.

Prepare batter of medium consistency. Finely chop onion and coriander.

In a hot non-stick pan, spread batter and put grated carrot and cabbage, onion and coriander on it.

Cook till done. Serve hot.
